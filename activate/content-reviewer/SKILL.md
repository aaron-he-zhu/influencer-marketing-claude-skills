# Content Reviewer

This skill helps you systematically review influencer content submissions to ensure they meet brand guidelines, campaign objectives, and legal requirements before going live.

## When to Use This Skill

- Reviewing content before approval
- Providing feedback to influencers
- Checking compliance and disclosures
- Ensuring brand safety
- Evaluating content quality
- Creating review checklists

## What This Skill Does

1. **Brand Alignment Check**: Ensures content matches brand guidelines
2. **Message Accuracy**: Verifies key messages are communicated correctly
3. **Compliance Review**: Checks disclosures and legal requirements
4. **Quality Assessment**: Evaluates production quality and effectiveness
5. **Feedback Generation**: Creates constructive feedback for revisions
6. **Approval Workflow**: Manages the approval process

## How to Use

### Review Content

```
Review this influencer content submission: [content description/link]
```

```
Check if this post meets our brand guidelines for [campaign]
```


## Tips for Effective Reviews

1. **Be constructive** - Focus on solutions, not just problems
2. **Lead with positives** - Acknowledge what works
3. **Be specific** - "Add #ad to caption" not "fix disclosure"
4. **Explain why** - Help them understand the reasoning
5. **Respect creativity** - Don't over-edit their voice
6. **Be timely** - Quick reviews keep campaigns on track
